A Little About Me

More than just a
student with books.

My name is Kolawole Abdullahi and I did not stumble into Health Information Management by accident. I chose this path because I saw how hospitals around me were drowning in paper, losing patient files, and making decisions based on incomplete data. That bothered me more than I can explain.

I want to be part of the generation that fixes that. Whether it is implementing better EHR systems, training staff on data governance or contributing to national health data policy, I am preparing myself for all of it.
